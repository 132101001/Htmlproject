DREAMCATCER

The Beginning of the End

"The Beginning of the End" is the first Japanese album and the first studio album by Dreamcatcher.
The album features a connection point in the story with their 4th mini album released in Korea. This suggests a larger narrative unfolding in Dreamcatcher's discography, adding depth to their storytelling.
In addition to new songs, the album includes previously released Japanese versions of their Korean songs "PIRI" and "What." The title track for the album is "Breaking Out."
The physical version of the album is available in three versions: a regular edition with a random trading card, and two limited editions - Type A (The End) and Type B (The Beginning). All three editions come with an event participation ticket, offering fans the opportunity to participate in related events.
"You and I (Japanese Ver.)" was pre-released on August 22, 2019, giving fans a taste of the Japanese version of this track ahead of the album's full release.
This album holds significance as it marks Dreamcatcher's entry into the Japanese market and showcases their storytelling prowess by connecting with their previous Korean releases.
